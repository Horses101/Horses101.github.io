Horses

The horses that I ride

Cinnamon

Cinnamon's full name is"Cimmanon Heart". She is very fussy at times and as her name suggests, she can be a little 'Spicy'. Her name refers to her personality and her Color. She is Cinnamon Brown in the right light.

Cinnamon responds well when I am riding her but sometimes, when she is fussy, she likes to go her own way. That's when I have to work to get her to listen again. Cinnamon is fun to ride and I like to give her carrots when she has been good. .

Dash

Dash is called that because he likes to run away and eat grass when he gets the chance. Dash is colored black and white. When i am cantoring, He likes to be lazy and cut the corners. He is easy to control and he is a little fussy. I like to ride Dash. He loves carrots too.
